WHAT IS GENTRIFICATION?

Gentrification is the placement of high-cost development in lower income areas. As these areas face modernization,
affordable housing becomes less available. Minority groups are most affected in the process as they have been forced
to relocate to impoverished, underdeveloped areas. This website will help educate users further on what gentrification
is, where it all started, and solutions to the issue.
