Martyrdom of the Seven Maccabees, 1863

The use of the word "martyr" is extremely prevalent here. Representing individuals whose lives were taken for what they believed in, this word holds significant meaning to the context of this piece. Sharif goes on to explain the association between martyr and casualty. Both of these words have a sense of ambiguity in their meanings. How did it happen? Why? By who?
